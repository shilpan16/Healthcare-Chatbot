About Us

Welcome to the Cognitive Wellness and Medical Guidance platform!
Our platform combines advanced AI technology with an intuitive interface to provide personalized health support. Whether you're exploring symptoms, seeking mental health resources, or finding nearby medical facilities, our chatbot is here to assist.

Key Features

  • Health Assistance: Get instant, reliable answers to health-related queries.
  • Disease Insights: Receive AI-powered analysis and early detection of potential conditions.
  • Find Medical Help: Locate nearby doctors, hospitals, and clinics effortlessly.
  • Mental Health Resources: Access guidance and tools for stress and anxiety management.
  • Completely Free: Enjoy full access to healthcare guidance without any cost.

Technologies Used

Our project leverages a robust stack of modern technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js
  • Health Data: JSON-based dataset
  • Database: MongoDB
