About the Hospital

Aware Global Hospital is a 300-bed multi-specialty hospital offering services in trauma, emergency, gastroenterology, cardiology, neurology, nephrology, and oncology. The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical staff, providing top-notch healthcare services to the community in L.B. Nagar and surrounding areas.

Address: L.B. Nagar, Hyderabad, India
